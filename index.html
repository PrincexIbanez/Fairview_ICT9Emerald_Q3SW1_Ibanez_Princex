Chainsaw Man

Aki Hayakawa (早はや川かわ アキ Hayakawa Aki?) Is the Deutrologist of Chainsaw Man, He is a Devil Hunter and seeks to kill the Gun Devil

Power (パワー Pawā?) is the tritagonist of Chainsaw Man, A Selfish Childish Fiend who seeks to live her life and protect her cat

Denji (デンジ?) is the titular protagonist, He was a poor child but ever since gaining the Chainsaw Heart he is going to chase his dreams
